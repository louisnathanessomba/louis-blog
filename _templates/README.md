# Templates Library

This folder contains reusable structures for posts across all divisions of the blog.  
Each template is unique to its section but follows a consistent professional style.

---

## Foundations
- **thesis-essay-template.md** → For essays in the Foundations division (Firmware AI thesis, vision statements).

---

## Applied Works
- **project-intro-template.md** → For project introductions (overview, context, objectives, roadmap).  
- **project-technical-template.md** → For technical details (architecture, components, implementation, testing).  
- **project-ethical-template.md** → For ethical–ecological reflections (human-centered, ecological, sustainability).

---

## Exploring Frontiers
- **independent-research-template.md** → For deep dives into mathematics, physics, chemistry, computer science, philosophy.  
- **companies-thread-template.md** → For comparative analyses of tech companies (Microsoft, Google, Tesla, etc.).

---

## Everyday Insights
- **student-life-tips-template.md** → For practical advice posts (productivity, study methods, balance).  
- **me-too-template.md** → For opinion/reflection posts on news and current events.

---

## About Me
- **about-me-template.md** → For biography, vision, philosophy, and contact posts.

---

## Usage
1. Copy the relevant template into the correct division folder.  
2. Replace placeholders (`[Insert Title]`, `[Insert Project Name]`, etc.) with actual content.  
3. Add metadata (`title`, `date`, `tags`) in the front matter.  
4. Expand sections with your own insights, code snippets, diagrams, or references.  

---

## Philosophy
Every template integrates **ethics and ecology** as core elements.  
This ensures that all posts — technical, personal, or reflective — align with the vision of **responsible innovation**.
