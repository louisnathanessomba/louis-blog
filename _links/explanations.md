# Link Usage Guide

## Home
Use in:
- Navigation bar
- Footer
- Any “Back to Home” button

## Foundations
Use in:
- Thesis essays
- Project intros (to link back to the thesis)
- Research posts referencing your core ideas

## Applied Works
Use in:
- Project intro → link to technical + ethical pages
- Technical page → link to intro + ethical page
- Ethical page → link to intro + technical page
- Foundations → link to applied examples
- Research → link to real-world applications

## Exploring Frontiers — Independently Search
Use in:
- Research posts linking to subfields (math, physics, etc.)
- Foundations → when referencing scientific principles
- Applied Works → when referencing theory behind a project

## Exploring Frontiers — Companies Threads
Use in:
- Company comparison posts
- Foundations → when referencing industry paradigms
- Ethics & Ecology → when analyzing corporate responsibility

## Ethics & Ecology
Use in:
- Project ethical pages
- Foundations → when discussing consequences
- Everyday Insights → when reflecting on sustainability

## Everyday Insights — Student Life Tips
Use in:
- Productivity posts
- Study method posts
- About Me → when discussing your student journey

## Everyday Insights — Me Too
Use in:
- Opinion posts
- Reflections on news
- Foundations → when connecting events to your thesis

## About Me
Use in:
- Navigation bar
- Footer
- Any post where you introduce yourself
- Contact references
